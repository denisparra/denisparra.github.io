Hi! I am Denis, Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, in the School of Engineering at PUC Chile. My research interests are Recommender Systems, Information Visualization and Analysis of Social Networks. If you want to collaborate on any of these topics, drop me an e-mail at dparra[at]ing[dot]puc[dot]cl.

If you want to work under my supervision as Master or PhD Student, check the graduate programs at the School of Engineering at PUC Chile here. Thanks for visiting my page!

First Semester 2014: Introduction to the Major in Computer Science. After a core of common courses in Engineering, undergrad students are PUC have to choose an area to obtain their BS. This is an amazing course, where students are exposed to the area of Computer Science in topics such as HCI, Web Development, Databases, Data Mining and ML, and Computational Complexity. Students have 4 reading assignments on themes such as PageRank (how Google ranks Web Pages) and Topic Models (a model to automatically clasify and summarize collections of documents) and they work on a series of assignments that involve Social Networks (Facebook, Twitter), Visualization tools (d3.js, raphael.js), and Prediction Algorithms (Logistic Regression, Decision trees, clustering, etc.)
